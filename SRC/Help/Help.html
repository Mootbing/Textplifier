How To Use

  1. Open the app (Source code or packaged)

  2. The top of the app is category info, for TextFX mode, it is the copied text of your clipboard

  3. The left side of the app is the "sidebar", where you can select a category

  4. The right side of the app is the "selector", where you can do actions based on the category selected in the "sidebar"

  5. You can select items in the "selector" by single clicking the option, it will be highlighted on the first click

  6. If you double click the element, it'll copy the result to your clipboard, for easy pasting. A message box at the bottom should appear to indicate if the action succeeded
